What is Web Design?

Web design is the art and science of creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. It involves a blend of creative design elements and technical skills to craft the layout, structure, and aesthetics of a website. Web designers use various tools, including HTML, CSS, and graphic design software, to ensure that a website not only looks visually appealing but also functions seamlessly across different devices and browsers. Effective web design encompasses considerations like user experience, accessibility, and responsiveness, making it a crucial discipline in today's digital age where websites serve as essential platforms for information, communication, and commerce.
